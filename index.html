About the Instructor

Mr. Kadaji is currently the Associate Director of Information Systems at Syracuse University Project Advance (SUPA). He joined SUPA in 2005 when he was hired to design a program management system, a project that soon grew into a full-time commitment. Today, he is responsible for the design, development and maintenance of Project Advance’s registration, records, billing, and administrative systems. Among the programs he has launched are Syllabus Maker and an Evaluation and Assessment (IES) tool.

Mr. Kadaji is also the co-founder and CTO of Canusia Solutions Inc., a software company based in Syracuse, NY that provides program management systems to higher educational institutions all over the US.
